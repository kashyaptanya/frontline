ABOUT US

Fast, Professional, Reliable

Frontline Finish was established in 2013 and rapidly became the preferred provider of auto body and paint services to new car dealerships and retail customers. We provide high-quality auto body and paint repairs at a budget-friendly price. Although we work with all major insurance carriers, our primary focus is to assist customers who prefer to pay "out of pocket". We understand the unique requirements of these customers and work with them to provide quality repairs at a reasonable price. We’re so confident in our work, we back all repairs with our industry-leading Lifetime Warranty.

OUR SERVICES

Auto Body Repair

Unless you have experienced a wreck on the highway, most vehicles do not require the services, and overinflated prices, of a collision center. Modest cosmetic damage, often the result of minor fender-benders or parking lot mishaps, is our specialty. Quite often, this type of damage can be repaired for less money than the insured driver’s deductible.

Auto Paint

Is your vehicle faded from sun damage or has the clearcoat degraded to the point where you no longer want to drive your car? Whether you would like to restore one panel, two panels, or the entire vehicle, we can restore your vehicle back to its original shine and luster! Our state of the art spray booth and computerized paint-mixing system delivers showroom quality results each and every job!
